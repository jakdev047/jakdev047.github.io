Mobile Application Developer

Vacancy

Not specific

Job Context

Object Data, Inc, located in New Jersey, USA is seeking experienced Mobile Application Developers to support our IT development team in New Jersey, USA

Job Responsibilities

  • Minimum 8+ years of Software development experience
  • 5+ years of experience in designing, building and implementing cross-platform mobile application solutions in both Android and iOS.
  • Demonstrable portfolio of released applications on the Apple App store and/or the Google Play store
  • In-depth knowledge of at least one programming language like React Native
  • In-depth knowledge of at least one cross-platform mobile application development tool like Xamarin, Ionic, or Appcelerator
  • Deep understanding of mobile software development methods, technologies, tools, and architectures (Objective-C, SWIFT, RESTful web services, etc.)
  • Familiarity with OOP design principles
  • Experience with Java/Kotlin, Google Play Services, Android Studio, and Xml layouts.
  • Working knowledge of database concepts and SQL
  • Working knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript is required
  • Solid knowledge working with Restful APIs and large datasets

Employment Status

Full-time

Educational Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Engineering, or related field is a MUST
  • Skills Required: Android application development, Apple App store, Google Play store, ionic framework, iOS Application Development, ObjectiveC, React Native

Additional Requirements

  • Age 25 to 40 years
  • Both males and females are allowed to apply
  • Excellent communication skills in English (written & spoken). MUST be fluent in English.
  • Good interpersonal skills required to interact with clients, technical staff, and third parties.
  • Software development experience with a US/European company/environment is highly desirable.
